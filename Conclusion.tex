\chapter{Conclusion}
\vspace{-1cm}

\hspace{0.5cm}Machine learning offers immense potential across industries, but its adoption is hindered by the steep technical expertise required for programming, data preprocessing, and model interpretation. Existing commercial AutoML platforms often remain expensive, proprietary, and operate as opaque "black boxes," creating a significant barrier for domain experts and small businesses seeking to extract meaningful insights from their data. To bridge this gap, the proposed ``InferX ML'' platform delivers a unified, fully open-source, no-code environment that automates the entire ML lifecycle. Built on a robust containerized microservices architecture, it seamlessly handles data ingestion, intelligent profiling, and asynchronous model training across a wide array of paradigms—including classification, regression, clustering, time-series forecasting, and image classification. What sets InferX ML apart is its emphasis on transparency and accessibility. By integrating AI-powered natural language goal analysis and comprehensive SHAP-based explainability, users receive accurate predictions alongside clear visualizations of the model's reasoning. Coupled with an auto-generated Streamlit prediction interface, InferX ML successfully democratizes AI. Ultimately, it serves as both a practical analytics tool and an educational incubator, empowering non-technical users to build and deploy production-ready models effortlessly.
